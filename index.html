About

Sim has tenacity, an incredible work-ethic, and most importantly creativity.  Many people believe that to be creative one must be endowed with a special gift. It’s the mystic of being an ‘artist.’  Magically, ideas pop into one’s head. But this isn’t exactly true. To be creative is to nurture relationships, to be observant, and be inquisitive. While I wish I could say that it was us, the Art & Digital Design faculty, that helped Sim to cultivate her creativity, I believe it’s more about the way she has spent her young life traveling and seeking out new experiences. Hard work alone doesn’t always mean a successful outcome but in Sim’s case it is the hard work with determination that has aided her in much of her academic success. Two words that I could use to describe Sim’s work are mature and sophisticated. Her work represents some of the strongest examples of work developed in the Art & Digital Design major.  Her determination, strong work-ethic and creativity are exemplary.

 

 - Wendy Romero,                                                     Associate Professor of Art, Loras College
