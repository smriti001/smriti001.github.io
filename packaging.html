                                                                                             Packaging

Pillano Craft Beer

A packaging design for a craft beer that has three different flavors including the lager, stout and lambic. It is brewed in Chicago and the target audience is the high end group of people who have a unique taste for beer. The three flavors lager, stout and lambic are represented by gold, silver and bronze respectively. The package is designed keeping in mind the size, portability and the aesthetic of the brand.
